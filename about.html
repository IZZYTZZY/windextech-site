About WindexTech

CRM and AI expertise.
Delivered for your business.

WindexTech is a specialist CRM and AI automation consultancy. We implement HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and Zoho — then build AI-powered automation on top so revenue teams focus on closing, not admin.

Our work spans CRM implementation, complex Salesforce CPQ builds, full platform migrations, RevOps strategy, multi-system integration, and AI agent development — for clients across SaaS, legal, property, staffing, telecoms, and data technology.

Every engagement starts with honest platform advice and a clear written strategy — then we build to match how your team actually works.
