FAQ

Common questions

Is the report really completely free?
Yes — the entire report. Your composite score, all 10 dimension findings and recommendations, the 30/60/90 day plan, the manual checklist, and the downloadable PDF. No payment details required, no hidden upgrade.
How long does it take?
Most portals are scanned in a couple of minutes. Larger portals with very high contact volumes may take a little longer — you'll see progress as each check runs.
Is my HubSpot data safe?
You create a read-only Private App token in your own HubSpot account and paste it directly into the tool. WindexTech never sees, stores, or logs your token or your portal data.
Do I need technical knowledge to set this up?
No. Creating a token takes about 2 minutes, and we show you exactly which boxes to tick, step by step, inside the tool.
What if I'm not on HubSpot?
The automated report currently covers HubSpot portals. If you're on another platform — or haven't picked one yet — book a free 15-minute call and we'll talk it through directly.
Will you try to sell me something afterwards?
Your report is yours to use however you like — with or without us. If it's useful, you can book a free call to talk through next steps. No pressure either way.
